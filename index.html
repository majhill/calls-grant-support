Why We Built This

Nonprofit leaders are too busy running their mission to read the landscape. Grant opportunities are publicly available — federal, foundation, corporate. Cycle dates are posted — nobody is hiding them. Eligibility requirements are written down — they just require patience to decode.

The money is sitting there waiting to be claimed. And most small nonprofits never get it — not because they don't deserve it — but because nobody took the time to read.

That's why CALLS built this.

What the Consultation Includes

Who This Is For

  • Registered nonprofits ready to pursue grant funding
  • Organizations with a clear mission but limited grant experience
  • Leaders too busy running programs to navigate the grant landscape
  • Nonprofits that have applied before but need better alignment and strategy
  • Organizations preparing for their first grant cycle
